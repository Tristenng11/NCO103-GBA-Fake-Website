Hundreds Gather in Peaceful Public Demonstration at City Centre

Singapore · Published today

A peaceful public demonstration took place this afternoon in the city centre, drawing several hundred participants who gathered to voice concerns about rising living costs and community support measures. The event remained calm and orderly, with participants holding signs, giving short speeches, and engaging in discussions along the designated route.

Police officers and safety marshals were present to guide foot traffic and ensure that the demonstration proceeded smoothly. Organisers emphasised that the gathering was intended to be constructive and respectful, encouraging participants to share their views responsibly.

Businesses in the surrounding area remained open, though some reported slightly heavier footfall as onlookers stopped to observe the event. Public transport services continued operating normally, with minor diversions implemented around the demonstration zone.

Authorities remind the public: Large gatherings should remain peaceful and follow designated routes to ensure safety for both participants and bystanders.

The demonstration concluded in the late afternoon, with organisers thanking attendees for maintaining a calm and respectful atmosphere throughout the event.
