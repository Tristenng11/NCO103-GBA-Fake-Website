Where You Can Spend Your CDC Vouchers

CDC vouchers can be used at thousands of participating merchants across Singapore, including hawker centres, heartland shops, and supermarkets.

Popular categories

  • Kopitiams and hawker stalls
  • Minimarts and provision shops
  • Barbers and salons
  • Family-run retail stores
  • Supermarkets (generic, non‑trademark)
