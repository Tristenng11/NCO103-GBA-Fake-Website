Public Holiday in 3 Days: Residents Gear Up for Long Weekend

Singapore · Published today

Singapore residents can look forward to a mid-week break, with a public holiday coming up in just three days’ time. Many families are already making plans for short staycations, family gatherings, and visits to local attractions.

Travel agencies and hotels have reported a spike in last-minute bookings, while heartland malls are preparing for heavier footfall as shoppers take advantage of sales and promotions timed to the long weekend.

Employers are reminding staff to submit any urgent work before the mid-week break, as some offices will operate with reduced manpower on the eve of the holiday. Public transport operators also expect higher passenger volumes during peak periods.

Key reminder: Essential services such as healthcare, public transport, and emergency response will continue to operate, but residents are advised to check operating hours for clinics, banks, and retail outlets in advance.

For those staying in, community clubs and public libraries will be running special programmes and activities, giving residents more options to relax and recharge without leaving the neighbourhood.
