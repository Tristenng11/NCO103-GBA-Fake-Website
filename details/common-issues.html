Common Issues When Claiming — And Fixes

Some residents may encounter login errors or delays during peak periods. Here are the most common issues and how to resolve them.

Common problems

  • SMS OTP not arriving
  • Session timeout
  • QR code not loading
  • Voucher link not opening

Fixes

  • Switch to mobile data or Wi-Fi
  • Restart the Singpass app
  • Try again during non-peak hours
